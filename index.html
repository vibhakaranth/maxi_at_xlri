    An ISO 9001-2000 certified organization, MAXI is probably the only committee in India to hold a Limca and a Guinness World Record.

    Our activities range from the cult classics - MAXI Fair to getting marketing Live Projects, from our insightful industry interaction series - Food For Thought to the battle of wits - Circus Maximus, from the first Section League of XLRI – Section Wars to the legendary clash ofmarketing titans - MAXI Bazaar

    Who are we?

    The Marketing Association of XLRI, or MAXI as it is fondly known, is the oldest committee on campus with a fifty year legacy.

    Our Mission

    Our mandate is simple - popularize the field of marketing at XLRI. MAXI strives to continually innovate and provide the best possible learning experience to the students.

    What we do

    We organize a mix of competitive events, talks, interaction sessions, conferences and of course, the renowned MAXI Fair.
